Art and Culture

  • Butterflies have appeared in art from 3500 years ago in ancient Egypt
  • The close association of butterflies with fire and warfare persisted into the Aztec civilisation
  • Butterflies are widely used in objects of art and jewellery
  • a butterfly was seen in Japan as the personification of a person's soul; whether they be living.
